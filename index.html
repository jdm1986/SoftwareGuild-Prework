The Thistle and Heather Inn

Nestled in the heart of the Highlands, the Thistle and Heather Inn offers traditional Scottish fare with a view. Enjoy wild boar, wood pigeon, and wild pheasant paired with local produce and bread, alongside the finest whiskies, ales, lagers, and ciders the Highlands and Hebrides have to offer.
